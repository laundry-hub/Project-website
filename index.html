The simple application enables customers request for a cleaning service online from any place of their convenience for example their homes or places of work where they use Postmates API to deliver laundry to desired service provider.

Problem statement

Dry cleaning, laundry and general cleaning services are some of the most vital services in Uganda since many people need this help due to different circumstances in which they can't ably carry them out of their own but as they search for these services there are many challenges they also face including inefficient ways of making payments, it's time exhausting having to move to the service Centre and returning to collect one's items(clothes) after the cleaning, there is also frequent misunderstanding between service providers and customers regarding failure to perform the service in time

Many cleaning service centers have been coming up in most urban centers around the country for example Kampala but these have still failed to meet all these issues that come with the service to any reasonable extent. This has made it difficult for many to blend in with the whole idea of cleaning services which has also led to losses by people who choose to open up cleaning service businesses

Create Delivery

On the home Page, the user provides a pickup address and a dropoff address which are parameters required by the postmates API. The response from the Postmates API returns the price for the delivery to occur and one can either cancel or continue with the transaction.

Create Transction

On being redirected, one may choose to cancel or continue with the delivery. To continue, Add more details to support the error proneness of the delivery.

Transactions dashboard

On creating transaction, the user is redirected to dashboard for transaction status.

Payment

After the delivery is completed, the service-provider then calls the customer to verify. On completion of cleaning laundry, the service-provider then calls the user to notify them after which they are required to pay. At the home route is provided a pay option which redirects to the pay Page One then fills in the required mobile money number and bill respectively in the input boxes.

Payment and database

If successful, The transaction details are stored in an online database hosted with MongoDb. User is redirected to the success page and is provided a link to return to the homepage for more transactions.
